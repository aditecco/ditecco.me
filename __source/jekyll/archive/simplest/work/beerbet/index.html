With Beerbet, betting on the hottest sport matches is easy and fun.

From the creator of Prezzipazzi, Beerbet was an experimental side-project that combined sport betting, virtual currencies and social graphs.

I was hired to build the visual side of the product, both identity & user interface.

UI Design

Bet category, calcio serie A View full-size

Prior to focusing on the UI, I needed to translate what was merely an app idea/prototype into something people could actually use.

The development team provided me with a written description of each page and its functionalities; from there I started visualizing the information architecture, then I wireframed a low-fi layout for each app view.

Once the wireframes were approved, the next step was turning them into high-fidelity mockups.
