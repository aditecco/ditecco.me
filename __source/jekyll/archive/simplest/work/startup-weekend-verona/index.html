Startup Weekend Verona

Startup Weekend is a 3-day hackaton for people who want to turn startup ideas into successful companies.

In 2012 I was part of the organizing team who brought Startup Weekend to Verona, for the first time ever in the city. Apart from taking care of my share of organizational tasks, I was in charge of the event's visual identity and communication.
